About DoBu Martial Arts

Building Confidence in Motion through High-Class Training

DoBu Martial Arts was founded by Mauricio Gomez (4th Dan Judo, 3rd Dan BJJ) with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a state-of-the-art training environment where students of all ages and abilities can master both the physical skills and the underlying philosophy of martial arts. We believe that true strength comes from technical proficiency, physical conditioning, and unwavering mental discipline. Our programs are designed not just to train fighters, but to build confident, focused, and resilient individuals.

Premium Training Environments

The Large, Matted Martial Arts Area

The spacious, professional-grade mat area is the central hub of all training at DoBu. We use high-density, shock-absorbent flooring, which is essential for ensuring the safety and longevity of our students' joints during throws, takedowns, and continuous grappling sessions. This expansive area allows us to conduct large classes and specialized drills without compromising safety or space.

The Fully-Equipped Strength & Conditioning Gym

We believe that peak martial arts performance requires more than just technical skill; it demands superior physical conditioning. Our dedicated gym is outfitted with modern, high-quality equipment, including free weights, squat racks, cardio machines, and functional training apparatus (kettlebells, battle ropes). This space is crucial for building the explosive power, core stability, and endurance needed to excel in combat sports.

The Sauna

Dedicated to accelerating post-training recovery, our traditional dry sauna offers numerous benefits for the dedicated athlete. The intense dry heat promotes detoxification, relaxes tight muscles, and helps reduce inflammation, making it an ideal way to unwind after a rigorous workout. Regular sauna use supports faster physical regeneration, ensuring you are ready for your next session.

The Steam Room

Our steam room provides a moist-heat alternative to aid in muscle recovery. The high humidity helps to open the airways and improve circulation, flushing metabolic waste from the muscles and reducing soreness. It offers a soothing, therapeutic environment, perfect for unwinding and preparing your body for optimal rest and repair.

Changing Rooms

We offer spacious and meticulously maintained changing rooms for both male and female students. Each room is equipped with secure lockers for storing personal belongings, ample bench seating, and dedicated space to prepare for or wind down after your training session. We prioritize cleanliness to ensure a fresh environment for everyone.

Showers

To help you refresh quickly and comfortably after intensive training, our facility provides individual, modern shower stalls. Each unit is private and maintained through a rigorous, daily cleaning schedule to ensure maximum hygiene. We feature high-flow, adjustable temperature controls and quality fixtures, allowing for a comfortable and quick transition from the mat back to your day.
